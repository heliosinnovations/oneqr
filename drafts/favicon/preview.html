Design Notes

  • All designs use brand colors: Orange #ff4d00, Background #fffef9, Dark #1a1a1a
  • SVG format allows perfect scaling at any size
  • Three finder patterns (corners) are the universal QR code identifier
  • Each variation tested at 16px, 32px, and 128px for clarity
  • Works on both light and dark browser themes
  • Rounded corners match modern app icon aesthetics
